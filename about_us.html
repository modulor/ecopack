About us

Founded in 2008, Eco Pack Green Box Ltd. is a privately held company headed by Yossi Heyman, a packaging veteran with over 30 years of experience.

The company markets its unique, patented flagship product; the Eco Pack SystemTM, to carton manufacturers, distributors, plastic rentals and pooling systems both nationally and globally, for resale to fresh produce retailers, farmers and agricultural exporters.

Eco Pack has partnerships in place with Langsam Industries, Best Pack and Yam Plast, each leading industry players with combined annual packaging revenues of over €40 million.

It is Eco Pack’s vision to make the Eco Pack System a global industry standard for the cleaner, greener, healthier transportation of fresh and processed foods.

Eco Pack is headquartered at Haharod 56, Yashresh , Israel.

Visión

“To become a global industry standard for the cleaner, greener, healthier transportation of fresh & processed foods.”

It is the Eco Pack vision to lead the packing industry into the next generation with simple, yet environmentally smart packaging products that can be Recycled and Reused, in order to Reduce waste and help preserve the planet.

Eco Pack is dedicated to intelligently recycling the world’s existing natural resources by keeping packaging economical, ecological, versatile and customizable, for use over and over… and over again.

Executive Team

Yossef Heyman, Founder

Yossef, a packaging veteran with over 30 years of experience, brings with him several packaging patents and unique innovations. Active in various agricultural packaging projects for a number of European food suppliers, Yossef is a partner in companies including: Langsam Industries, Best Pack and Yam Plast, each leading industry players with combined annual packaging revenues of over €40 million.

Prior to establishing Eco Pack, Yossef managed several packaging and recycling facilities, transportation companies, agriculture trade businesses and over 30

tray and box service centers.

Manuel Eduardo Reyes Cabal, COO & EVP

Mr. Reyes is a senior executive with 24 years of progressive and diverse leadership experience and proven strategic planning success in the retail Industry.

Prior to joining Eco Pack, he served as WalMart’s VP of Global Food Sourcing for the Eastern Seaboard and Central America. During his 7-year tenure with Walmart he served also as VP, GMM Fresh Foods and Vertical at Wal-mex (Walmart’s sister company) and as VP, Director Merchandising Manager Produce and Floral, for WalMart US, leading the largest produce business in the world with 3,868 stores, $8B+ in sales and supporting 42 US Distribution centers.

Prior to joining Walmart, Mr. Reyes served in various executive positions with Costco.

Ron Okman, Vice President

Ron brings with him over 20 years of experience in senior management, marketing and business development. Prior to joining Eco Pack, he served as Head of the Business Division at Tamhash Metal & Electricity Industries, where he successfully reorganized company structure to bring a 17% raise in company growth. Prior to that, Ron was CEO at A. Alexandrovitz, a leading supplier of Engineering Plastics, Pipeline Systems, Fittings, Valves and Rubber products, where he significantly increased both sales turnover and global company revenues. Ron also brings with him a wealth of experience in the Environmental & Energy sector from his time spent as Business Development Manager at Madei Taas, a privately owned company dealing in environmental protection technologies.
